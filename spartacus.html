Spartacus - EAU DE PARFUM 50 ML

Inspired By Terre D'Hermes

Spartacus is a timeless fragrance that captures the strength and courage of a true warrior. With a bright and uplifting opening of orange and grapefruit, this fragrance quickly transports you to the battlefields of Ancient Rome. The addition of flint in the middle note adds a smoky, yet earthy tone to the mix, while the base of woodsy notes and oak moss provide a solid and masculine foundation. Benzoin brings a warm sweetness to the scent and helps to enhance the overall aroma.

Miss Independent

Miss Independent is a Floral Aldehyde fragrance designed to honor independent and hardworking women. The top notes of Aldehydes, Ylang-Ylang, Neroli, Bergamot, and Peach give the perfume an energizing and uplifting ambiance, while the middle notes of Iris, Jasmine, Rose, and Lily-of-the-Valley combine to create a unique floral bouquet. The base notes of Sandalwood, Vanilla, Oakmoss, Vetiver, and Patchouli add depth and complexity to the scent, creating an aroma that is at once sophisticated and exciting. Miss Independent is a fragrance that celebrates the strength and beauty of independent women, and its complex yet subtle scent is sure to make an impression.

Alpha

Alpha is an adventurous fragrance for men that captures the unique spirit of exploration and discovery. It has an invigorating, aromatic scent with aquatic and fresh undertones. The top notes of bergamot and pepper give a sharp and slightly spicy kick, while the middle notes of Lavender, Pink Pepper, Vetiver, Patchouli, Geranium, and Elemi evoke a more complex and layered scent. The base notes of Ambroxan, Cedar, and Labdanum add a woody and earthy depth to the aroma. Altogether, Alpha is a captivating blend that will make you feel daring and empowered as you explore the world around you.
